Blindfold

Keep the mare blindfolded as much as possible and use the tightest option that still allow them to do what is required of them. eg.

  • When stalled, opaque
  • When carting, unblinded
  • Whip circle, medium to thin
  • Traverse, unblinded
  • Flower, medium to thin
  • Hanging around, medium to thin

About me

My name is Five, I've been active in the pony play community since 2022, and mare since about the same time, at SnR, then at Tall Tails.

I have over the few years formed some opinions on how I like to be handled, and how to handle others. These are my opinions, and they are not necessarily the best or most correct, but they are my opinions.

If you wish to have a chat, I'm on Discord as fivedoll, or in Second Life as i.doll.
