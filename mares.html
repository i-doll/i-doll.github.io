Blindfold

Keep the mare blindfolded as much as possible and use the tightest option that still allow them to do what is required of them. eg.

  • When stalled, opaque
  • When carting, unblinded
  • Whip circle, medium to thin
  • Traverse, unblinded
  • Flower, medium to thin
  • Hanging around, medium to thin
